About Soul Frames

General Info

Thank you for using Soul Frames! We are always happy to have more developers or frame experts join the cause and make this app better, if you want to contribute, hit us up through one of the contact links below! Most of the frame data has been gathered from character discord spread sheets and 8 way run. Please keep in mind that there is a chance that some values might not be accurate or up to date. Please let us know if you find any bugs or errors.

I created the base for Soul Frames over the course of about 6 months using Ionic and Angular before releasing the beta on Google Play. From that point onwards multiple people started to join in and contribute.

The apps icon and all the character art was created by my good friend. She's an artists who mainly does fighting game drawings and chibi merch, so give her a follow.

Starting fromthe beta release Ray joined the development team and is implementing new functionality to the app. He has previous experience with frame data applications and I'm really happy to have him on board. Check out what he's up to from the link below.

Get in Touch

Join our discord server and connect with the community. We have a section for bug/error reports and feedback on here. So if you want to report anything or just hang out, please feel free join!

Follow us on twitter and stay up to date with the apps development.
