About Soul Frames

General Info

The game is still pretty new and frame measurements are still in progress, data will be updated constantly. Please let us know if you find any bugs or errors.

I created the base for Soul Frames over the course of about 6 months using Ionic and Angular. If you want to have a chat or check out my other projects, feel free to follow me on twitter.

The apps icon and all the character art was created by my good friend. She's an artists who mainly does fighting game drawings and chibi merch, so give her a follow.

From the beta release and onwards Jaggers joined the development team and is implementing new functionality to the app. He has previous experience with frame data applications and I'm really happy to have him on board. Check out what he's up to from the link below.
