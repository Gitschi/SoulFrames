General Info

The game is still pretty new and frame measurements are still in progress, for that reason the app does not contain any actual frame data yet! I will add this data as I find contributors and the data becomes available. For the time being, the app can be used for the move lists and extensive filter functionality.

I created Soul Frames over the course of about 6 months using Ionic and Angular. If you want to have a chat or check out my other projects, feel free to follow me on twitter.

The apps icon was created by my good friend. She's an artists who mainly does fighting game drawings and pixel art, so give her a follow.
